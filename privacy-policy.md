
# Privacy Policy for WORD UNWIND

**Effective Date:** March 22, 2026

Rajana Devassykutty ("we," "our," or "us") developed **WORD UNWIND** as a mobile game application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

## Information We Collect

WORD UNWIND does not collect personal information directly from users through the app.

## Gameplay Data

The app may store gameplay-related information locally on your device, including:

- game progress
- scores


This information is stored only to support app functionality and improve the user experience.

## Third-Party Services

WORD UNWIND may use a third-party service:

- **SubWords API hosted in Microsoft Azure** — the app may call a remote endpoint to validate word candidates and retrieve valid words during gameplay. If the request fails or times out after 15 seconds, the app falls back to a local word list. This API is used only to support gameplay functionality and is not intended to collect personal information from users.

## How We Use Information

Any locally stored information is used only to:

- save gameplay progress
- preserve user settings and preferences
- support the normal functioning of the app

## Advertising

WORD UNWIND does not display third-party advertisements.

## Children’s Privacy

WORD UNWIND may be used by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, except as permitted by applicable law and platform requirements.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal information.

## Data Retention

To the extent any data is stored or processed, it is retained only for as long as necessary to operate, maintain, and improve the app, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

## Your Choices

You may stop all use of the app at any time by uninstalling it from your device.
